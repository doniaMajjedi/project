Who we are

Choufli Hal, developed by second-year engineering students at TekUp in Tunisia, is a versatile multiservice platform that connects local service providers with individuals and businesses in need. Our platform is designed to simplify access to high-quality local services, ranging from cleaning and gardening to beauty and well-being, as well as healthcare and various handyman services. Choufli Hal aims to facilitate connections between service providers and customers in Tunisia through an online platform, providing an opportunity for service providers to expand their reach and grow their businesses. Emphasizing job creation and empowerment of local service providers, Choufli Hal is committed to fostering economic opportunities. Whether you're seeking additional income through job opportunities or require assistance with small tasks, Choufli Hal is the go-to platform. If you're a service provider looking for job opportunities, our platform streamlines your search by sending job alerts directly to your email, notifying you of relevant opportunities based on your skills and location. Similar to the models of Frizbiz in France, Choufli Hal prioritizes transparency by showcasing providers' profiles, including skills, experience, and community ratings. The platform ensures trust through a comprehensive jobbing insurance coverage. So, whether you're a customer looking for services or a service provider seeking opportunities, Choufli Hal is here to simplify your life and connect you with the right solutions.
